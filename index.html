Connection Session

A connection session is an opportunity for healing through shared presence. A typical session will last an hour. Come with a specific issue to explore or simply an openness.

Healing happens through connection. To connect to your center, to what you are feeling in the moment, and to share that feeling with an empathic listener is healing.

I invite you to join me to see what needs to be seen and feel what needs to be felt ~ to settle into the present moment, ground the body, relax the mind and open the heart.

By connecting to the physical, mental, emotional and spiritual aspects of yourself, harmony is developed. Through connection we can find our way to health, happiness and expansion.

My personal practice for creating connection draws from neuroscience, yoga, qi gong, mindfulness and deep listening.

Sessions are 1 hour long ~ $60
